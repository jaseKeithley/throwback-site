Get a Ride

Not driving anymore? Not a problem. Senior Ride Nashville will find a ride for you - to the doctor, grocery store,
beauty shop, pharmacy and everywhere in between. We’ll connect you with one of our volunteers or one of our
partners like ride share, public transit or paratransit. And you’ll have the opportunity to meet a new friend!

Read our frequently asked questions here.

“The drivers are so kind and helpful. It’s like having one of my children help me. I feel so safe, and am grateful for such loving, professional help that allows me to stay in my home. Senior Ride Nashville has been a blessing.”
  1. Become a member (There is a $25 annual membership fee and rides cost $6 per round-trip ride.)
  2. Be a Davidson County resident and located in one of our current services areas
  3. Be 60 years of age or older
  4. Be able to transfer in and out of a car easily (If you use a wheelchair, it must be able to fit in the trunk of a car.)
  5. No longer be able to drive or drive very seldomly
  6. Complete an application and submit your initial payment (We will mail you an application after you fill out the form below.
    Applicants are asked to submit a deposit fee of $49. Once accepted into the program, this fee will cover the
    first $25 annual membership fee as well as four rides. Applicants who are unable to pay the full fee are asked
    to call the office to discuss further.)
  7. Complete a home visit assessment (We will contact you after we receive your completed application to schedule an assessment.)
