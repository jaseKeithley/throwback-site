About

Jase Keithley is a engineer, sound designer, and programmer. He currently attends Belmont University in Nashville, TN. While at Belmont, Jase obtained an internship with Dark Horse Recording and currently interns there as one of their staff engineers.

Sound Design

Through learning by professionals who have worked on A list movies, Jase can help you with any post-production needs.

Recording

Jase provides reamping servies, tracking, as well as overdubs. All for a flat fee.

Mixing

With over three years of professional mixing experience, Jase can turn your idea into a on the charts hit!
